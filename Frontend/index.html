IELTS Coaching

International English Language Testing System (IELTS) is designed to assess a student’s English proficiency for study, work, and migration abroad. It focuses on Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking skills. A good IELTS score increases chances of global education and career opportunities.

2 WEEKS PREPARATION

TOEFL Coaching

Test of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL) measures academic English skills required for higher education abroad. It evaluates how well students understand and use English in a university environment. TOEFL is widely accepted by international universities and institutions. High TOEFL scores help students secure admissions and scholarships.

2 WEEKS PREPARATION

OET Coaching

Occupational English Test (OET) is specially designed for healthcare professionals. It focuses on medical workplace communication in English. OET is required for doctors, nurses, and healthcare workers in many countries. A good OET score helps professionals work and settle abroad confidently.
