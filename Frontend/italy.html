Study in Italy

Why Study in Italy

Italy combines historic academic excellence, strong arts & design programs, and vibrant student life.

Historic, Prestigious Universities

Centuries-old universities with internationally recognised degrees.

Arts, Design & Fashion Excellence

Top programs in fashion, design and architecture with strong industry links.

Affordable Tuition & Scholarships

Low tuition at many public universities; scholarships available.

Living Costs & Part‑time Work

Manageable living costs and part‑time work/internship options.

English-taught Programs & Support

Many English-taught degrees and strong international student support.

Top Universities in Italy

University of Bologna

Bologna, Emilia-Romagna

The oldest university in the world, renowned for law, humanities, and science.

Law Humanities Science Engineering

Sapienza University of Rome

Rome, Lazio

One of Europe's largest universities, strong in engineering, medicine, and social sciences.

Engineering Medicine Social Sciences Computer Science

Politecnico di Milano

Milan, Lombardy

Italy's top technical university, famous for architecture, engineering, and design.

Engineering Architecture Design Computer Science

University of Padua

Padua, Veneto

Historic university with a strong reputation in science, medicine, and law.

Science Medicine Law Engineering

University of Milan

Milan, Lombardy

Leading research university, especially in medicine, law, and the sciences.

Medicine Law Sciences Business
